Understanding the ReLU Loss Landscape

This project explores the mathematical structure of the loss surface in shallow (1-hidden-layer) ReLU neural networks. We analyze minima, maxima, and saddle points via gradients and Hessians, visualize input-space partitioning, and track gradient norms under different optimizers.
